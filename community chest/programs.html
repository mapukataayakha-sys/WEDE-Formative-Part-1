Funding Programs

Grants & Support for NGOs

We provide grants to non-profit organisations working in marginalized communities across South Africa. Our funding programmes focus on four key areas.

Health

We fund organisations providing primary health care, community health services, and health promotion programmes. Our focus areas include:

  • Primary health care clinics
  • HIV/AIDS prevention and support
  • Maternal and child health
  • Community health workers
  • Mental health services
Apply for Health Funding

Education

We support education programmes from early childhood development through to adult literacy. Our focus areas include:

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD)
  • Primary and secondary education support
  • Adult literacy programmes
  • Skills training and vocational education
  • Scholarship programmes
Apply for Education Funding

Community Development

We fund grassroots organisations working to empower communities and build social capital. Our focus areas include:

  • Community mobilisation
  • Youth development programmes
  • Women empowerment initiatives
  • Rights-based programmes
  • Community safety
Apply for Community Development Funding

Income Generation

We support economic empowerment programmes that create sustainable livelihoods. Our focus areas include:

  • Small business development
  • Job creation initiatives
  • Cooperatives and social enterprises
  • Financial literacy
  • Agricultural programmes
Apply for Income Generation Funding

Capacity Building

In addition to grants, we offer training and development programmes for funded organisations:

Governance Training

Board development, fiduciary responsibilities, and organisational leadership.

Financial Management

Budgeting, financial controls, and reporting for NGOs.

Programme Management

Project cycle management, monitoring, and evaluation.

Fundraising

Grant writing, donor relations, and sustainability planning.

Eligibility

To apply for funding, your organisation must:

  • Be a registered non-profit organisation (NPO) or non-governmental organisation (NGO)
  • Have a proven track record of at least 2 years
  • Demonstrate financial stability and good governance
  • Work in one of our focus areas
  • Serve disadvantaged communities in South Africa
