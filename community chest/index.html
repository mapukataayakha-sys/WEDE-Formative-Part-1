Our Legacy

Founded in 1928 to support community organizations in Cape Town, Community Chest has grown into a leading funder and capacity builder for NGOs across South Africa.

We provide grants, training, and partnerships to grassroots organizations working in marginalized communities. Our approach combines financial support with capacity building.

Mission: To improve lives by mobilizing communities, business, and government to advance the common good.

Why We Exist

Civil society organizations work at the frontlines of social change, yet often lack access to capital and resources. We bridge this gap.

Our Approach

We believe solving South Africa's challenges requires participation from all sectors. Our work invests in developing active citizens across civil society, business, and government.
