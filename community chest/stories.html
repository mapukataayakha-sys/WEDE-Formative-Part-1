Skills Academy for Youth

Cape Town

Since 2015, Skills Academy for Youth has been providing IT and technical training to young people from disadvantaged communities. With funding from Community Chest, they have expanded their programme to train over 500 youth annually.

The results speak for themselves: 85% of graduates secure employment within 6 months of completing their training.

"Community Chest funding allowed us to scale from 50 students to over 500. They didn't just give us money - they helped us build our organisational capacity."

- Thabo Moeletsi, Executive Director

Education

Little Lights ECD Centres

Khayelitsha

Little Lights operates six Early Childhood Development centres in Khayelitsha, providing quality education to over 300 children aged 0-6 years.

Their specialised approach focuses on school readiness, with 92% of children achieving school readiness milestones - well above the national average.

"We believe every child deserves a strong start. Community Chest helps us give that to children who otherwise would be left behind."

- Sarah Johnson, Founder

Health

Community Health Workers Programme

Gugulethu

This network of 150+ community health workers provides primary health care services to over 25,000 residents in underserved areas.

Funded by Community Chest since 2010, the programme has become a model for community health delivery across South Africa.

"As community health workers, we are the bridge between the clinic and the community. Community Chest saw our potential before anyone else."

- Nokuthula Ndlovu, Programme Coordinator
